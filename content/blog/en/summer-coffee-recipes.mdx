## Recipe 1: Iced Arab

"Iced Arab" lets you travel to Arabia. Famous for its sweets in which almonds cannot be missed and where dates and spices are emblematic. It is the perfect drink to cool off after a long day at the pool.

-   Orange peel
-   150ml Almond drink

## **Blockchain and Ethical Finance: our alliance for social impact**

At **EthicHub**, we believe in the transformative power of blockchain technology to democratize access to financing. That is why we are excited to announce that, together with [**Bybit**](https://www.bybit.com/en/) and the [**Blockchain for Good Alliance (BGA)**](https://www.chainforgood.org/), we have secured a **$1 million investment** aimed at strengthening our ethical credit lines for smallholder coffee farmers.

## **An innovative financial model for smallholder coffee farmers**

Thanks to our alliance with **Bybit**, facilitated by **BGA**, we continue to consolidate a **blockchain-based financing model** that provides **fair and transparent credit** to smallholder farmers. Through the exclusive **Bybit Pool**, we offer access to financing without traditional intermediaries or abusive lending conditions, allowing coffee farmers to grow sustainably.

With this model, we ensure that capital flows directly to farming communities, providing them with financial tools that drive economic and social development.

## Pilot successfully completed

In October 2021, we inaugurated a pilot project to finance small coffee farmers in Honduras. **Domingo Rosales, president of CODICAFE requested 15,000 dollars (USD)** to carry out the harvest but did not have assets to guarantee the loans. But there were Ethixholders, in this ecosystem willing to stake their Ethix to guarantee the entry of this new community. 6 Ethixholders covered the required guarantee in just a few hours since its launch, and CODICAFE entered our platform.

![honduras_ethichub_finca](https://uploads-ssl.webflow.com/649c3ddd37399d5d10230640/64a416997f138c0345873568_honduras_ethichub_finca.jpeg)

During this time, CODICAFE uploaded three projects, achieving the desired funding. Money that has been used to create wealth and excellent specialty coffee.  
  
And best of all, the coffee growers of CODICAFE have honored their commitment by repaying the loan with promised interest. **Now we can say that the pilot in Honduras has been a complete success.**
